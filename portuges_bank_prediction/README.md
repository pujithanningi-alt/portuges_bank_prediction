Portuguese Bank Marketing Prediction
Executive Summary
This project analyzes a dataset from a Portuguese banking institution to predict whether a customer will subscribe to a term deposit. By leveraging machine learning techniques, we identified key factors driving customer conversion and developed a predictive model to help the bank optimize its marketing campaigns and reduce operational costs.

Project Goal
The primary objective was to build a classification model to identify potential subscribers. By effectively targeting customers most likely to say "yes," the bank can focus its resources on high-value leads rather than broad-spectrum marketing.
